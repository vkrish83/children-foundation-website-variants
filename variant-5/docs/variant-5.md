# Variant 5 — Community Movement Foundation

## Vision

Variant 5 should feel completely different from:

* V2 Professional Foundation
* V3 Human Storytelling
* V4 Impact Report

This variant should feel like a movement.

Not a charity.

Not a report.

Not a corporate foundation.

The feeling should be:

* Energy
* Participation
* Community
* Optimism
* Momentum
* Action

---

## Inspiration

Use the images inside:

references/

as visual inspiration.

Prefer:

* bold typography
* asymmetrical layouts
* overlapping sections
* editorial spacing
* large photography
* modern nonprofit design

Avoid:

* dashboard layouts
* report cards
* metric grids everywhere
* traditional NGO templates
* corporate CSR styling
