## Design Rules

Follow existing V5 pages.

Use:

- bold typography
- dark navy sections
- orange accents
- teal accents
- asymmetrical layouts
- large photography
- movement energy

Avoid:

- governance dashboards
- reporting layouts
- executive profile pages
